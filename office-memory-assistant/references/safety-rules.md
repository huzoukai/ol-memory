# Safety Rules

## Never Invent

Do not invent these without source support:

- customer names
- project amounts
- project years
- certifications
- patents
- awards
- company rankings
- team size
- named partners
- case outcomes
- legal or compliance claims

If missing, write `缺信息` or ask the user.

## Leader-Shared Materials

Leader-shared videos, articles, screenshots, or comments are preference signals only. They can guide angle, emphasis, tone, and examples the leader may like. They cannot prove company facts, customer results, awards, certifications, amounts, rankings, or legal claims.

## External Use

For bid, customer, public website, government, or investor-facing content:

- prefer conservative wording
- mark unsupported claims
- ask before exposing customer names
- avoid "行业领先", "全国第一", "革命性", "全链路赋能", and similar unverifiable claims unless the memory library explicitly supports them

## Local Privacy

- Do not upload memory files.
- Do not commit `OL-Memory/`, legacy `my-office-memory/`, or legacy `office-memory-data/` to git.
- Do not write user facts into the skill installation directory.
- Keep learning confirmation explicit.

## Sensitive Patterns To Flag

Validation and review should flag likely phone numbers, emails, ID-like long numbers, money amounts, customer names marked confidential, and certifications without evidence.
